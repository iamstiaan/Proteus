01 — Brand Character

Proto the Protean

Proto is the heart of Proteus Kids Sports. A shape-shifting, chameleon-inspired mascot drawn from the mythology of Proteus — the ancient sea god of change — Proto embodies adaptability, growth, and the idea that every child has unlimited potential. Proto isn't just a logo decoration; Proto is a living brand character who appears across every program, platform, and piece of merchandise.

Gradient Usage Note
The Proto Gradient (green → teal → blue) is reserved for Proto the mascot, the primary "P" icon, and hero-level design moments. It should never be used on body text or as a generic background fill. Sport-specific accent colours — orange, yellow, purple, red — are used sparingly as single-colour highlights, never blended into the gradient system.

04 — Guidelines

Logo Usage Rules

Consistent, correct logo usage protects the integrity of the Proteus brand. The following dos and don'ts apply to all internal teams, licensees, partner schools, and suppliers.

✅ Always Do
  • Use approved logo files from the brand asset library
  • Maintain minimum clear space on all sides
  • Use the dark variant on navy or dark photography
  • Use the icon-only lockup when space is under 25mm wide
  • Apply the circular badge on round merch surfaces
  • Ensure sufficient contrast — minimum 4.5:1 ratio
  • Use the icon gradient exactly as supplied (never recolour)
🚫 Never Do
  • Stretch, squeeze, or distort the logo proportions
  • Recreate the wordmark in a different typeface
  • Place the logo on a clashing or low-contrast background
  • Add drop shadows, glows, or unauthorised effects
  • Rotate or tilt the logo at any angle
  • Use the full wordmark at sizes below the minimum
  • Combine the logo with other mascots or brand marks

05 — Usage Contexts

Digital, Print & Merchandise Applications

The Proteus visual identity has been designed to flex across every touchpoint — from a phone screen to a sports bag, from a classroom certificate to a social media thumbnail. The following applications are approved and actively deployed.

📱
Digital Platforms
Website, app UI, booking pages, email headers, digital report cards. Use the dark lockup on navy; icon-only at favicon size.
📲
Social Media
Profile images use icon-only circular variant. Post templates use full lockup at top-left with Proto centre-stage. Holiday promo banners use full gradient treatment.
📄
Print & Signage
Banners, flyers, program booklets, venue signage. Full primary lockup at minimum 25mm. CMYK colour profiles supplied separately.
🏅
Certificates & Reports
Achievement, milestone, and program-completion certificates. Sport-specific Proto variant appears alongside the logo. Progress report templates use the light lockup.
⭐
Stickers & Stamps
Collectible sticker series uses sport Proto variants with value-word rings (Teamwork, Effort, Courage, Focus). Reward stamps use the circular badge lockup.
👕
Merchandise
T-shirts, caps, water bottles, bags, keyrings. Icon-only on small items; full lockup on apparel chest/back. Embroidery uses single-colour navy or white version.
Embroidery & Screen Print

Always use the single-colour white or navy version for embroidery. The gradient cannot be faithfully reproduced in thread. For screen printing under 30mm, use icon-only.

File Formats Supplied

SVG (scalable, web), PNG (transparent background, screen), EPS (print production), PDF (presentations). All variants included in the brand asset pack.
